Nirmaankara Customer Cancellation Policy

Nirmaankara connects customers with service providers (SPs) in the construction industry, helping them discover and select an SP based on criteria that matter most to them. We take pride in offering transparent and reliable construction services, investing significant effort in understanding customer requirements, providing designs, and delivering detailed documentation.

In the unlikely event that a customer chooses to cancel the project after making a partial payment of the booking amount, the customer must notify Wingherry TECHNOLOGIES PVT. LTD. Private Limited ("Nirmaankara") of their intent to cancel. This notification should be sent from the email address registered with Nirmaankara, either via email to nirmaankara@gmail.com or by registered post with acknowledgment due, clearly specifying the reason(s) for the cancellation. Upon receiving such notice, Nirmaankara will review the cancellation on a case-by-case basis, following the principles outlined below.

For cancellations made beyond 48 hours after payment of the booking amount, a non-refundable onboarding fee equal to 1% of the Construction Cost, along with the applicable service fee corresponding to the stage of service, will be retained.

The Construction Cost refers to the total cost of the project before any discounts. The service fee will be calculated based on the latest Construction Cost approved by the customer.

Any additional costs incurred by the contractor during the construction stage for resource mobilization, beyond the 2% stage-wise fees mentioned earlier, will be charged on an actual basis. In the event of a dispute between the customer and the contractor regarding these charges, Nirmaankara will make the final decision on the amounts chargeable and/or any extra time required.

Any services not listed in the schedule will also be charged on an actual basis. The fees mentioned above are inclusive of taxes.

For any cancellations after the project has started, the cancellation amount will be deducted as per the terms of the agreement between Nirmaankara and the customer

If architectural or structural designs are completed, or if data like digital surveys or soil tests are collected, this information will be shared only according to the schedule.

If the costs incurred by Nirmaankara in providing these services exceed the amount paid by the customer, the customer must pay the difference within 30 business days of receiving written notice from Nirmaankara. If the payment is not made within this period, an interest rate of 18% per annum will be applied until the full amount, including interest, is received.

Nirmaankara will transfer any refund amount, after making necessary deductions and adjustments, to the bank account provided by the customer within 30 business days from the date of mutual agreement on the final refund amount. Cash refunds are not possible.

Nirmaankara reserves the right to modify the cancellation policy at any time without prior notice. The customer will be bound by any such changes, and Nirmaankaraâ€™s decision will be final and binding on all parties.
