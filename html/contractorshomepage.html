Q3.How can i get the leads on NIRMAANKARA? keyboard_arrow_down
Lead allows you to track and manage potential clients or projects. You can receive leads from various cities, view hot classifieds, and assign leads
Q4.How do I follow up on leads? keyboard_arrow_down

Q5.How do I create a quotation for a project? keyboard_arrow_down

Q7.How do I manage Work Status? keyboard_arrow_down
