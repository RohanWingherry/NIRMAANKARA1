    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION'S (FAQ's)
    Q1. What property types are available in Nirmaankara? keyboard_arrow_down
    You can choose from various property types, including Residential (Flats, Single-Family Homes,Townhouses), Apartments (Furnished, Unfurnished, Studio), Commercial (Office Spaces, Stores, Hotels), Villas (Luxury, Eco-Friendly), and Land (Residential, Commercial, Agricultural).
    Q2. Can I schedule a visit to a property? keyboard_arrow_down
    Yes, you can schedule a visit through the property detail page. Availability will depend on the property and the owner's schedule. Additionally, the seller provides a vehicle option for visits.
    Q3.How can I report a property that seems suspicious or fraudulent? keyboard_arrow_down
    Most platforms have a reporting mechanism. Look for a "Report" button on the listing or contact customer support to address any concerns.
    Q4.Is the Nirmaankara app available on mobile? keyboard_arrow_down
    No, the Nirmaankara app is currently not available on mobile devices. You can access the platform through the website for all property-related services.
    Q5.Can I apply for a mortgage or financing through the platform? keyboard_arrow_down
    No, our platform does not currently support direct applications for mortgages or financing. For financing options, please check the property detail page or the financial services section for potential partnerships with lenders.
    Q6.Are there any additional fees I should be aware of? keyboard_arrow_down
    No, there are no additional fees for our service. However, if you exceed your visit limit, you may need to purchase a package for extra visits. Be sure to check for any property-specific fees like maintenance or taxes.
    Q7.What happens if I exceed the free visit limit? keyboard_arrow_down
    After five free visits, you'll receive an alert stating, “YOUR FREE SCHEDULED VISITS HAVE EXCEEDED THE LIMIT. PLEASE SELECT A PAYMENT OPTION.” You'll have the option to go back or choose a payment package.
    Q8. How can I view images and videos of a property? keyboard_arrow_down
    Property detail pages feature a dedicated section for images and videos, allowing you to visually explore the property.
