FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION'S (FAQ's)
Q1. What kinds of contractors are available on Nirmaankara? keyboard_arrow_down
Nirmaankara provides a comprehensive range of contractors for both general and specialized needs.
General Contractors: These handle a variety of projects, including commercial apartments, renovations, complexes, villas, residential apartments, and ventures.
Specialized Contractors: This category includes experts in specific areas such as plumbing, electrical work, interior design, carpentry, painting, demolition, flooring, and roofing.
Q2. Can I request a contractor to visit my site before providing an estimate? keyboard_arrow_down
Yes, you can request a contractor to visit your site before providing an estimate. This allows them to assess the project more accurately and offer you a detailed quote.
Q3. What should I do if I have a complaint about the work or if a contractor doesn’t show up for a scheduled appointment? keyboard_arrow_down
You should take the following actions if you have a problem regarding the job or if a contractor fails to show up for an appointment: Contact the Contractor: Make a direct contact to voice your issues and find a solution. Document Everything: All correspondence should be kept on file, with dates, times, and conversational specifics. Notify Nirmaankara:You can utilize the feedback function to report a problem if it hasn't been resolved. Subsequent Action: Until your problem is resolved, maintain contact with Nirmaankara and the contractor.
Q4. How long is the quotation valid?What happens if the project goes over budget? keyboard_arrow_down
Quotations are typically valid for 3Months(Depends on contractor), but it s better to confirm with the contractor initially. If the project exceeds the budget, communicate with the contractor to discuss the reasons and options. Review the contract for procedures regarding budget overruns.
Q5. Can I negotiate prices with contractors? keyboard_arrow_down
You can haggle over rates with contractors, yes. It s normal to talk about costs and maybe make adjustments depending on your project s scope and budget. Just be really explicit in the conversation about what you need and want
Q6. What if I want to hire multiple contractors for different tasks? keyboard_arrow_down
you can do so by selecting and engaging separate contractors for each specific service. The platform allows you to manage multiple contractors for various aspects of your project, ensuring that each task is handled by the appropriate specialist.
Q7. What If I want to add features during construction? keyboard_arrow_down
Let the contractor know as soon as possible if you wish to add features while the project is being built. Plan ahead for probable adjustments to the budget and schedule, and make sure that all changes are recorded in writing to prevent confusion.
Q8. Are there any hidden fees I should be aware of? keyboard_arrow_down
No, there are no hidden fees to be aware of. The contractor s quotation should provide a comprehensive breakdown of all prices
Q9. Can I change the contractor of I m not satisfied? keyboard_arrow_down
Yes, if you are dissatisfied with the contractor, you can seek a change; however, it is critical that you understand your contract conditions, convey your issues effectively, and collaborate with the platform or service to identify an appropriate replacement.
Q10. Is there a fee for customers to use this service? keyboard_arrow_down
No, there are no fees for customers to use our service. You can find, reach out, and connect with contractors completely free of charge, and all services are offered at no cost.
